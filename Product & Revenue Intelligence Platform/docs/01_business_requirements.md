---

# Project Overview

The goal of this project is to design and develop an enterprise-level analytics platform that enables business stakeholders to monitor product performance, customer behavior, revenue trends, seller performance, and pricing insights through interactive dashboards and advanced analytics.

---

# Business Problem

The company generates a large amount of transactional data every day but lacks a centralized analytics platform to support strategic and operational decision-making.

Decision makers cannot easily identify:

- High-performing products
- Revenue drivers
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Seller performance
- Pricing opportunities
- Business growth opportunities

---

# Business Objectives

- Improve business visibility.
- Monitor revenue performance.
- Analyze product performance.
- Understand customer behavior.
- Evaluate seller performance.
- Support pricing decisions.
- Deliver executive dashboards.
