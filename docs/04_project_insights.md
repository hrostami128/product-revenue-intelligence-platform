# Business Insights

## Revenue

- Revenue is concentrated in a limited number of product categories.
- A small number of customers contribute a significant share of total revenue.

## Customers

- Customer distribution varies across states.
- Average order value differs by customer segment and region.

## Products

- Some product categories generate high revenue with relatively low sales volume.
- Freight costs vary considerably across categories.

## Sellers

- Seller performance is uneven across regions.
- A limited number of sellers account for a substantial portion of revenue.
